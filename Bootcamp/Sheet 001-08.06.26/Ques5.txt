N-Queens
Difficulty: HARD | Max Score: 75
The N-Queens puzzle is the problem of placing n queens on an N x N chessboard such that no two queens attack each other.

Given an integer N, return the number of distinct solutions to the N-Queens puzzle.



 

Example 1:

Input: N = 4
Output: 2
Explanation: There exist two distinct solutions to the 4-queens puzzle : [[".Q..","...Q","Q...","..Q."],["..Q.","Q...","...Q",".Q.."]]


Example 2:

Input: N = 1
Output: 1
Explanation: There exist one distinct solutions to the 1-queen puzzle : [["Q"]]
 

Constraints:

1 <= N <= 9
