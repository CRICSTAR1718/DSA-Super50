Level Order Traversal of Binary Tree
Difficulty: MEDIUM | Max Score: 50
You are given Root node of a Binary Tree.

Your task is to return Level Order Traversal of the Tree.



 

Example:

Input: 
      5
     / \
    2   9
     \   \
      6   3
     / \
    12  8

Output: [[5],[2,9],[6,3],[12,8]]
Explanation: 
 

Constraints:

0 <= No. of nodes <= 104
