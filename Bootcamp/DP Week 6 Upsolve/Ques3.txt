Rod Cutting
Difficulty: EASY | Max Score: 25
Given a rod of length N and an array P  of size N+1.

Pi  denotes the price of a piece of length i. Determine the maximum amount obtained by cutting the rod into pieces and selling the pieces.





 Input Format:

The first line contains an integer N(length of rod).
Second line contains N+1 space separated integers denoting P0 , P1 , P2 , ........., PN


Output Format:

The only integer that you have to print is the maximum amount obtained by cutting the rod into pieces.




Example 1:

Input: 8
0 1 5 8 9 10 17 17 20
Output: 22
Explanation: The maximum obtainable value is 22 by cutting in two pieces of lengths 2 and 6, i.e., 5 + 17 = 22.


Example 2:

Input: 5
0 2 4 6 8 15
Output: 15
Explanation: The optimal move is to sell the entire rod of length 5 for a profit of 15.
 

Constraints:

1 <= N <= 103
0 <= Pi <= 105
P0 = 0
